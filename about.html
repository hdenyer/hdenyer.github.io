Holden Denyer is a master of mediocrity.

Born and raised in Maryland, Denyer is inspired by what he finds around him - which is definitely not the worst, but also definitely not the best. He finds occasional passion in creativity, to which he expels and experiments with it in various mediums.

Denyer is a perfectionist, his greatest curse. His efforts are placed most in the little details, a gift to those who look. Denyer's work continues to take on new forms, each striving to be his best piece yet.
